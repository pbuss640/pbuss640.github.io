Why Cats?

Cats are so nice and will curl up in your lap after a hard day at work. They purr and sleep until it seems like they will do nothing else. But BEWARE! They are often known to bite or scratch the hand that feeds them.

These lovable creature of danger are fond of playing with you. This can include a toy or just the hand that was petting them just a second before. Either way, it will be attacked and disarmed with much disgrace.

Cats may seem very dangerous, but they are a lot more fun than dogs. They keep you on your toes, almost literally as they are always under your feet. Get a cat, you will thank me later.
